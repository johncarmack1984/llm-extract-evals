Willow Creek Solar is a 175 MW photovoltaic facility developed by Cascade Renewables in the NYISO control area. It interconnects at the Marcy 345 kV substation under queue number NY-2023-117. A facilities study is complete; network upgrade costs are estimated at $22.6 million. The requested in-service date is May 15, 2028, and the project is active in the queue.
